About Us

Veenu Ghai
Proprietor

In 2012, I started my journey with tiffin services from my home kitchen—not because I was naturally good at cooking, as many women are, but because I had the fortune to understand the texture of ingredients, the art of pairing the right foods, and, most importantly, the magic of flavors. I have always loved giving a fresh, modern twist to age-old ghar ka khaana, which led me on an exciting gastronomical journey of transforming boring recipes into delightful creations for my clients to relish. Pursuing this passion was not an easy decision; it meant stepping away from a well-paying job to venture into the unknown. Yet, I’m grateful I took that leap, as it has brought me to where I am today. At FOODYUM, we promise to deliver something exclusive, with a ghar jaisa feel, that’s not just delicious but healthy too!

Why
Foodyum ?

At our establishment, we prioritize quality and freshness in every dish. We procure fresh vegetables daily and use only premium-quality ingredients, including desi ghee, Amul butter, and filtered oil as our cooking mediums. Our homemade multigrain tawa rotis and carefully selected basmati rice (a mix of dubar and mogra) reflect our commitment to wholesome, authentic flavors. All our cooking is done with purified RO water, ensuring cleanliness and safety. With experienced chefs, trained staff, and over a decade of culinary expertise, we deliver a multicuisine menu featuring creative recipes. We take pride in serving clean, homemade food with no added preservatives—just pure, flavorful goodness.
